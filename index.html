About

I am a PhD Student at the Robotics and Perception Group, led by Prof. Davide Scaramuzza. My research interests lie at the intersection of computer vision, robotics and neuroscience. Currently, I am working on my PhD degree focusing on computer vision for event cameras.

I received my masters degree in Robotics, Systems and Control from ETH Zurich under the supervision of Prof. John Lygeros.
